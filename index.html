Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about ordering, shipping, or materials? Find quick answers below.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?
Our standard wholesale MOQ ranges from 50 to 100 yards depending on the fabric collection (e.g., 50 yards for luxury silk, 100 yards for Egyptian cotton). For smaller custom boutiques, we offer sample yardage options.
Do you offer free fabric sample catalogs?
Yes! We provide free sample swatches to registered retail clothing brands, designers, and manufacturers. You can request a swatch book through our Contact page or message us directly on WhatsApp.
Do you ship nationwide in Pakistan?
Absolutely. We ship nationwide from our Lahore central showroom and warehouses via reliable cargo networks (e.g., TCS, Leopard, local truck freights) at discounted corporate shipping rates.
Can you dye or weave custom patterns?
Yes, we specialize in custom manufacturing! We can weave custom designs, print bespoke patterns, or dyestuff fabrics to exact Pantone color codes. Custom weaving runs typically require a minimum of 1,000 yards.
