Our Story

A Lahore business built on three decades of fabric knowledge, honest dealing, and a genuine love for the craft.

Wholesale Fabric Suppliers Since 1995

Anas Ahmad Fabrics started in 1995 at a small shop in the Shadman textile market in Lahore. Back then, the focus was simple: find good fabric, price it fairly, and deliver it on time. That approach has not changed.

Over the years, we have built relationships with mills across Pakistan and beyond — sourcing cotton, silk, linen, and wool directly so we can offer consistent quality at reasonable wholesale rates.

Today, we supply boutiques, fashion designers, tailors, and small manufacturers from our Lahore showroom. We are not the biggest fabric house in Pakistan, but we take pride in being reliable, responsive, and easy to work with.

How We Got Here

The real story behind Anas Ahmad Fabrics — no marketing language, just the truth.

Where It Started

In 1995, Anas Ahmad opened a small fabric stall in Shadman, Lahore. The market was competitive, but the focus on quality fabric and straightforward dealing built a loyal base of regular buyers — tailors, boutique owners, and small manufacturers who came back because they knew what they would get.

What We Learned

Running a fabric business taught us that buyers do not want to be oversold. They want to know the GSM, the width, the origin, and the price — and they want a supplier who can answer the phone when there's a problem. That is the kind of business we have tried to build.

Where We Are Now

Today we operate from our Sharja Centre showroom in Shadman, Lahore. We carry cottons, silks, linens, wools, velvets, and brocades. Clients can visit in person, request swatches, or message us on WhatsApp to discuss their requirements before placing a bulk order.

How We Work

A few things we genuinely believe make a difference when you are sourcing fabric for a real production run.

Fabric You Can Trust

Every roll in our showroom is checked for weave density, colour accuracy, and GSM before it goes out. If something does not meet the standard, it does not leave our warehouse.

Transparent Pricing

We share per-yard rates, MOQ requirements, and bulk discounts upfront. No guesswork, no surprise charges. If you want to compare options, we will help you do that honestly.

Consistent Supply

We maintain a good stock of our core fabrics so production runs do not get held up. For seasonal items, we give buyers advance notice so you can plan your orders in time.
